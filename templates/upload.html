Model Limitations:

  • Only accepts JPG and PNG images
  • Trained to recognize only 50 specific game series (listed below)
  • Built entirely from scratch under hardware constraints
  • Trained on just 25,000 images
  • Model accuracy does not exceed 55%
  • Confidence rarely exceeds 6%
  • Cannot recognize games outside the trained series — it will still force a classification

About the Project:

This machine learning project was built by Rishav Dhara in the 3rd year of B.Tech CSE (Data Science). No mentorship, tutorials, or guidance was used — just self-taught coding, AI tools, and raw passion.

The dataset of 25,000 images was entirely collected manually by one student over a challenging week of downloading, screenshotting, and cleaning. No transfer learning was used; the model was built completely from scratch using ML libraries.

Despite its limitations, this is a passion project — one that came alive thanks to the support of fellow gamers who contributed screenshots. Thank you to everyone who helped. Your love for games made this possible.
